About Yetay

Learn more about the vision behind Yetay Bible Competition

Our Vision & Mission

Yetay is a divine initiative birthed to bring young minds together from various denominations through healthy Bible competition. We believe in promoting biblical literacy, unity, and growth among participants.

Since inception, Yetay has served as a platform where spiritual excellence meets friendly rivalry, where hearts burn with love for the Word, and where youths bond beyond doctrine.

Story So far

The journey began in 2024, under the Banner Unto Nations Foundation. What started as a small local quiz has grown into a landmark interdenominational event, drawing participants from far and near.
